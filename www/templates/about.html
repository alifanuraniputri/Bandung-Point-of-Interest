# Bandung-Point-of-Interest


This is a hybrid Ionic mobile apps. Show public's interest places around Bandung, Indonesia. This apps is a part of final project bachelor degree by Alifa Nurani Putri 13511074


The public interest is extracted with multi-channel crowdsourcing, data text and number from several social media.
